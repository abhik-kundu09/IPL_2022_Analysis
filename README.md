## 📖 Project Overview

The **Indian Premier League (IPL)** is one of the world's most exciting and competitive T20 cricket tournaments. This project performs an in-depth analysis of the **IPL 2022 season** using match-level data.

The goal of this project is to explore team performances, player contributions, toss strategies, venue trends, and match-winning factors through **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

✔ Analyze team performances throughout the tournament

✔ Understand toss decision patterns and their impact

✔ Identify top-performing batsmen and bowlers

✔ Explore Player of the Match trends

✔ Study venue-wise match distributions

✔ Generate meaningful cricket insights through data visualization

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🥇 Most Successful Team

**Gujarat Titans** emerged as the most dominant team of IPL 2022 with the highest number of victories.

### 🎲 Toss Impact

Approximately **48.65%** of matches were won by the team that also won the toss, indicating that toss advantage alone was not enough to guarantee success.

### 🏏 Highest Individual Score

**Quinton de Kock** registered the highest individual score of the season with an incredible **140 runs**.

### ⭐ Most Player of the Match Awards

**Kuldeep Yadav** received the highest number of Player of the Match awards.

### 🎯 Best Bowling Performances

Outstanding bowling spells were delivered by:

- Jasprit Bumrah (5/10)
- Wanindu Hasaranga (5/18)
- Umran Malik (5/25)
- Yuzvendra Chahal (5/40)
